How it works

Symbiosis by design: the clownfish and the anemone.

A clownfish thrives inside the anemone's protection; the anemone thrives on what the clownfish brings. Nemo runs the same way: the digital bank lives inside a licensed partner's regulatory shelter, and brings it customers, deposits and lending income.

The anemone — licence holder

Partner bank / MFI / DCP

Holds the licence, the prudential capital and the settlement accounts. Keeps its core of record if it wants.

⇄
The clownfish — Nemo tenant

One tenant = one neobank

Its own customers, products, ledgers, brand and reports — isolated on a shared platform. One installation runs many banks.

Launching a neobank is configuration, not a project:

1 · Provision tenant  →  2 · Pick a market pack (currency, rails, KYC, tax, regulator)  →  3 · Configure products  →  4 · Apply brand pack  →  a running, compliant bank.

06 / 13

Deploy anywhere

One click to a bank — on our cloud, your cloud, or your basement.

One-command install

The entire platform — services, databases, event bus, observability — ships as a single Kubernetes package with sane defaults. Sandbox bank in under an hour.

On-premise & air-gapped

Data-residency laws in our markets make on-prem a feature, not a compromise. Offline bundles, licence activation, no phone-home required.

Day-2 automated

Zero-downtime upgrades, gated migrations, backups with tested recovery objectives, multi-node HA. Banks don't take maintenance windows.

Config over code — the iron rule. A new country is a market pack; a new savings product is product-factory data; a new brand is a theme. Nothing a regulator or product manager changes requires an engineer.

07 / 13

AI at the core

The bank mostly runs itself. Humans set policy and review by exception.

AI decides the money
  • Straight-through credit — apply → score → disburse with zero human touch for in-policy cases, explainable declines for regulators
  • Fraud & AML — ML detection with AI triage that drafts cases and SARs for officer sign-off
  • Collections — risk-based strategy, best time & channel to contact, hardship detection
AI runs the system
  • Ops agents — watch telemetry and business metrics, diagnose event lag and reconciliation breaks, execute runbooks behind approval gates
  • Customer AI — support chat with real banking actions, personalised nudges, churn prediction
  • Governed — every model registered, monitored for drift, bias-tested, overridable, with a kill switch

One decision engine logs every automated call the bank makes, with its reasons — automation a bank's risk committee can sign.
